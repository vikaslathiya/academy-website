About Us

  Chanakya Academy is located in a panoramic atmosphere in the Kamrej village, Surat. The Academy is managed by Shree Gopal Lathiya. Academy is located about 3 kms away to the west of Surat-Kamrej road. With reasonable & affordable fee structure, the Academy provides a wonderful environment for the full expression of personality.

     Considerable importance is given for the development of skills, character, manners & etiquette, leadership qualities & public speaking. Academy is spread in 20 acres of land with a built up area of 8 lac squares feet having separate residential facility for boys & girls. Foresight of our trustee members is to create Modern Vidya Sankul in the most economical cost & providing education to every mass and class of society.Most of trustees associated with the trust are settled in Surat. Other activity of the trust is to run two medical centre managing a group of 30 doctors. Here the medicines are available at 20 % consessional rates, medical treatment is given by specialist & super specialist doctors at the lowest expenditure.
